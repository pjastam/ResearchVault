`Thesisit.ai` is an AI tool designed to assist with various aspects of thesis writing. The primary function of `Thesisit.ai` is to support researchers in drafting and formatting their theses, although it is important to note that the tool should not replace a researcher's own critical thinking skills. This caution was emphasized by an academic expert reviewing the tool, who stressed that while `Thesisit.ai` can help streamline certain tasks, it is not a substitute for the user's own intellectual evaluation and analysis.

The tool offers functionalities that include drafting sections of the thesis, formatting it according to specific guidelines, and possibly assisting with peer review support. However, users are warned against over-reliance on the AI features, particularly when it comes to critical aspects of research such as synthesizing information or drawing conclusions.

`Thesisit.ai` is part of a suite of tools designed to facilitate academic writing and research, including `Libright.app` for manuscript formatting and peer review support, and `Ascent`, which allows users to upload papers, compare them, and synthesize information. Each of these tools aims to enhance efficiency in the academic process but must be used judiciously to ensure that researchers maintain their analytical capabilities.

While these AI tools can be valuable resources, it is essential for users to remember that they are aids rather than substitutes for the core elements of academic research. The ethical implications and the potential risk of losing critical thinking skills underscore the need for balanced usage.
