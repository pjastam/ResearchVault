## Ascent

`Ascent` is an AI tool designed to assist researchers in synthesizing information from academic papers. This tool allows users to upload multiple papers, select fields of interest, and then engage with the content through features like `chat with the papers` and compare functionality.

### Key Features:
- **Upload Papers:** Users can upload their research papers into the system.
- **Select Fields of Interest:** The tool enables users to specify areas they are interested in within their research.
- **Chat with Papers:** This feature facilitates interaction with the content of the uploaded papers, likely through a conversational interface.
- **Compare Papers:** The ability to compare different papers based on various criteria is available.
- **Citation Generator:** Ascent includes a citation generator to help users create accurate citations effortlessly.
- **Graph View:** A unique graph view offers a visual representation of the relationships and connections between different papers and topics.

### Use Cases:
Ascent is particularly useful for researchers who need to stay current with recent advancements in their field. By allowing users to chat with and compare papers, it helps streamline the process of reading and synthesizing large amounts of academic literature.

### Ethical Considerations:
While Ascent provides significant assistance to researchers, it is essential to maintain critical thinking skills and not rely solely on AI-generated summaries. Researchers should use tools like Ascent as aids rather than substitutes for their own work.
