# Libright.app

Libright.app is an AI tool designed to assist academics with manuscript formatting and the peer review process. The tool offers a range of functionalities aimed at streamlining academic writing tasks.

<!-- olw-auto: single-source — cross-reference recommended -->

## Features

- **Upload your manuscript:** Users can upload their manuscripts for processing.
- **Journal Formatting:** Libright.app reformats the manuscript to match specific journal requirements.
- **Writing Support:** The tool supports writing or editing within Microsoft Word.
- **Peer Review Assistance:** It helps manage and facilitate peer review processes.
- **Cover Letters:** Users can draft cover letters using the tool.
- **Reporting Guidelines:** Libright.app assists with adherence to reporting guidelines.

## Workflow

The typical workflow involves uploading a manuscript, reformatting it for a specific journal, and utilizing various support features for writing, editing, peer review, cover letters, and reporting guidelines.

## Ethical Considerations

While Libright.app provides valuable assistance to academics, it is important not to rely solely on such tools for critical thinking or research synthesis. The tool should be used as an aid rather than a replacement for the user's own work and critical evaluation.
